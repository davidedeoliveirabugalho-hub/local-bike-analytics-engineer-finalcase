{% docs mrt_sales_by_product_daily %}

This model generates a daily summary of product sales, including the following metrics:

Granularity: product + date

- Total daily orders per product
- Total unique customers per product
- Total quantity sold
- Daily revenue

This enables the analysis of product performance at the daily level, making it easy to identify top-performing and underperforming products, analyze trends by category and brand, and detect seasonal patterns.
